About Us

The Civic Welfare Training Service (CWTS) at North Eastern Mindanao State University (NEMSU) - Tandag Campus is a vital component of the National Service Training Program (NSTP), as mandated by Republic Act 9163. Our program is designed to immerse students in activities that contribute to the general welfare and betterment of life for our community members. We aim to build civic consciousness and patriotism in the youth by developing their spirit of service and commitment to social responsibility.
